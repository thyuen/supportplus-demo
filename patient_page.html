New

NI Nurse Chat

A calm, guided symptom triage experience placed inside the patient portal. This is a static UI preview based on the AI Nurse Chat page so you can drop it into GitHub and continue iterating from here.

Nurse Chat - Symptom Triage
Chat to AI Nurse 24/7 about symptoms and get advice. You can escalate to a human nurse when needed.
⚠ If symptoms are severe or rapidly worsening, call emergency services or go to the Emergency Department now.
Quick start
AI Nurse
Hi, I’m the AI Nurse. I can help with symptom guidance and triage. Please describe what you’re experiencing. If this feels urgent or severe, call emergency services now.
Patient
I’ve had a fever since last night and I’m starting to feel more breathless when I walk.
AI Nurse
Thanks for telling me. Fever together with increasing breathlessness needs prompt review. Please check your temperature, note when the breathlessness started, and contact a clinician urgently today. If you are struggling to speak, feel drowsy, have chest pain, or lips look blue, seek emergency care immediately.
Nurse notified and will talk to you shortly (Ticket #2048)
Why this works
  • Places the new NI Nurse Chat entry inside the same left navigation pattern as the current patient portal.
  • Keeps the main frame familiar while upgrading it with a clearer card layout and stronger action hierarchy.
  • Preserves a safe healthcare tone with a highly visible escalation banner and obvious nurse handoff action.
Suggested next step

When you are ready, this static shell can be connected to the React logic from the original AiNurseChatPage.tsx component. For now, it behaves like a polished design preview that fits your existing portal frame.
